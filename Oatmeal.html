Ingreditents
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1/2 cup steel cut oats
  • 2 spoonfuls flaxseed meal
  • 1 spoonful brown sugar
  • 1 spoonful cinnamon
  • 1 cup wild blueberries
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts
Directions
  1. Pour the cup of almond milk into the stove top pot and bring to boil
  2. Once boiling turn the heat to low and add the 1/2 cup steel cut oats
  3. Stir until the oats have soaked up all the almond milk
  4. Add the flaxseed meal, brown sugar and cinnamon and stir together
  5. Add the blueberried and walnuts and stir
