Minestrone Soup

  • 2 pounds potatoes
  • 1 pound carrots
  • 1 pound celery
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cans kidney beans
  • 2 cans garbanzo beans
  • 1 pound frozen chopped spinach
  1. Chop up the potatoes
  2. Chop up the onion
  3. Chop up the carrots
  4. Chop up the celery
  5. Chop up the zucchini
  6. Put all the veggies in the crockpot
